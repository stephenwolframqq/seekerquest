# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

Seeker Quest respects your privacy. This policy explains how data is handled.

---

## 📊 Information We Collect

We may collect:

- Wallet address (for blockchain interactions)
- Quest participation data
- Basic app usage data

We do NOT collect:

- Personal identity information
- Contacts or private files
- Precise location data

---

## 🔗 Blockchain Data

- On-chain actions are recorded on the Solana blockchain
- Blockchain data is public and immutable
- We do not control blockchain storage

---

## 📢 Advertising

- Advertisements are displayed within the app
- We do not sell personal data to advertisers
- Ads are not personalized using sensitive user data

---

## 💰 Reward Distribution

- Reward calculations may use participation data
- No guarantee of earnings is provided

---

## 🔐 Data Storage

- Most data is stored locally or minimally processed
- Wallet interactions are handled via user-controlled wallets

---

## 🛑 Third-Party Services

We may interact with:

- Solana blockchain
- Wallet providers

We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

---

## 👶 Children’s Privacy

Not intended for children under 13.

## 🔄 Updates

We may update this policy at any time.
