# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to our application (“App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application, which is built on the Solana blockchain and requires wallet connection.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect
2.1 Wallet Information

When you connect your wallet, we may collect:

Your public wallet address
Blockchain transaction data related to your interactions with the App

Note: We do not have access to your private keys or control over your wallet.

2.2 Usage Data

We may collect information about how you use the App, including:

Tasks completed
Activity timestamps
Interaction logs
Device and browser type (if applicable)
2.3 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information such as:

IP address
Device identifiers
Cookies or similar tracking technologies (if applicable)
3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

Provide and maintain the App
Track task completion and rewards
Verify user activity on-chain
Improve user experience
Prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized activities
Comply with legal obligations
4. Blockchain Data

Due to the nature of blockchain technology:

Transactions you perform may be publicly visible on the Solana blockchain
We cannot modify or delete blockchain data
Your wallet address may be permanently recorded on-chain
5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information in the following cases:

With service providers who help operate the App
To comply with legal requirements
To protect our rights and prevent fraud
In connection with a business transfer (e.g., merger or acquisition)
6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to:

Provide the App services
Fulfill legal obligations
Resolve disputes

Blockchain data is retained permanently and cannot be deleted.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However:

No system is 100% secure
You are responsible for securing your wallet and private keys
8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

Access your data
Request correction or deletion
Object to processing

Note: Some data (e.g., blockchain records) cannot be altered or deleted.

9. Third-Party Services

Our App may interact with third-party services such as:

Wallet providers
Blockchain explorers
Analytics tools

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with an updated “Last updated” date.
